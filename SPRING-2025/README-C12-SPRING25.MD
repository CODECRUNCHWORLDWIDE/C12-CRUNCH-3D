# C12 Crunch 3D

Welcome to **C12 Crunch 3D: CAD & Modeling**! This interactive course introduces students to On-Shape, a 3D CAD software used in engineering, product design, and manufacturing. Participants will gain hands-on experience in 3D modeling and assemblies. The course covers essential OnShape tools and workflows, culminating in a final real-world design project.

---

## 🔗 Getting Started  

To get the most out of this course, please follow the steps below to set up your OnShape account:

1. Search “Sign up for OnShape” or click [here](https://www.onshape.com/en/sign-up). 
2. Fill out the information with any name, any last name, and any email.
3. For use, select either Education or Fun.
4. Continue with the steps to fill out your account information.
5. You now have access to OnShape!
